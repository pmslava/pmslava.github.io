Top 55 Useful AI Tools

February 12, 2024 11-minute read
automatization • design • it • marketing • programming • ai • technology

The list of useful AI tools is inspired by the Howfinity video “I Tried 1200 AI Tools - These Are the Top 50”.

1. Jasper AI by OpenAI Link to heading

Jasper AI, a pioneer in generative AI for content creation, assists in crafting marketing content with the backing of GPT technology from OpenAI.

  • Category: Content Creation and Writing Assistance.
  • Strengths: Intuitive content generation.
  • Weaknesses: May require user edits for precision.

2. QuillBot Link to heading

QuillBot, a paraphrasing tool, rewrites sentences, paragraphs, and articles, making content writing and improvement straightforward.

  • Category: Content Creation and Writing Assistance.
  • Strengths: Effective rewriting capabilities.
  • Weaknesses: Dependent on original text quality.

3. Notion AI Link to heading

Notion AI enhances the Notion workspace platform, offering features for improving writing, adjusting tone, and generating content like blog posts and to-do lists.

  • Category: Content Creation and Writing Assistance.
  • Strengths: Seamless Notion integration.
  • Weaknesses: Limited to Notion users.

4. GitHub Copilot Link to heading

GitHub Copilot is an AI-powered code completion tool that accelerates development processes and improves code quality for developers.

  • Category: Content Creation and Writing Assistance.
  • Strengths: Accelerates development.
  • Weaknesses: Can produce inaccurate suggestions.

5. DeepL Link to heading

DeepL offers real-time translation into over 30 languages, simplifying the process of translating documents and texts.

  • Category: Content Creation and Writing Assistance.
  • Strengths: High translation accuracy.
  • Weaknesses: Limited by language pair quality.

6. Adobe Express Link to heading

Adobe Express is an all-in-one design tool with a library of generative AI tools for creating images, templates, and designs.

  • Category: Design and Image Editing.
  • Strengths: Extensive resource library.
  • Weaknesses: May be complex for beginners.

7. Microsoft Designer Link to heading

Microsoft Designer generates designs from text prompts, offering a full design platform with customization features.
