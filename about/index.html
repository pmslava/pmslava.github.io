About

“PM” is a special acronym for me. The letter P reflects three main areas of experience for me: Product, Project, and Process. The letter M stands for Manager, which is my main profession.

My name is Slava, and I can:

  • create products,
  • manage projects,
  • analyze processes,
  • and provide trainings.

In all these four areas, I have results that I am not ashamed of. You can find examples of my work on the Projects page of my website. Although large teams contributed significantly to the results, my part in each of the projects is also there.

Recently, I’ve been working as a Product Owner, responsible for developing a product that will compete against well-established solutions in the market. In my role, I communicate with investors, clients, technical directors, analysts, designers, marketers, sellers, and team leaders. I’m involved in all stages of the product development process, from market research and MVP development to technical support and user experience study.

If you would like to learn more about me, you can download my CV.

Five years ago, I switched from Windows to Linux and have enjoyed studying the system ever since. I love open-source software and try to use it wherever possible. In my free time, I enjoy classical and jazz music, cycling, and tennis.
